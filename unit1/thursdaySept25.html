Basic Text Formatting

Use the strong tag or the b tag to make something bold

The Difference Between Strong and Bold

The Strong tag is an HTML semantic tag. There is a meaning behind using the strong tag. What that meaning is: Tells the browser there is something important about the text inside that tag.

The B tag, means nothing. It simply bolds text and thats all.

The em Tag and the i Tag

The em Tag and the i Tag make the text inside of them itallic.Again, the difference is the em Tag is a semantic tag. The i Tag just turns the text itallic.

The u tag

The u tag simply underlines your text, no semantic meaning.
